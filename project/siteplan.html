Site Name

KLUGH Stores

This name highlights a unique brand identity for an online shop and is short, memorable, and easy to recognize.

Optional domain : klughstores.com

Site Purpose

KLUGH Stores is an e-commerce website that showcases featured products, categories, and promotions while providing clear paths to purchase and customer support. The site will include a home page for discovery, a Buy Now page for shopping and checkout, an About page to build trust in the brand, and a Contact Us page for questions and help.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find today’s featured deals on KLUGH Stores?
  • How do I contact customer support about an order or return?

Color Schema

Primary: Deep Teal (#0D4B4C) Used for headings, header background, and buttons.
Accent: Warm Sand (#F2C57C) Used for highlights, cards, and callouts.
Neutral: Slate (#1F2A30) Used for body text and borders.

Typography

  • Headings: "Poppins", sans-serif
  • Body: "Merriweather", serif
