About Us

Why we created this website

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves to hear the beautiful, inspiring stories of the Bible told in a way that speaks to their heart. Too often, these ancient stories feel distant or hard to understand for young readers.

That's why we created this space — to retell six beloved Bible stories in warm, accessible language that children aged 4–12 can read alone, or share with a parent or teacher.

Our goal is simple: to spark wonder, nurture faith, and help children see that these stories are not just from the past — they speak to us today.
